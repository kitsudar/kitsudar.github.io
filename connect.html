Connect Mobile App

The Problem

Parents of school age children can find it difficult to connect with their children’s teachers and other parents in their classroom.

The Solution

A vision for the app ‘Connect’ is for parents to be able to communicate with the teachers and other parents more effectively and bringing school community together in one place.

The Project

A competitive analysis was conducted to review and compare online sites and applications in the relevant marketplace. The goal was to identify any opportunities or gaps that aligned with our app’s value proposition that might allow for a quick win using a “Lean” approach for releasing a minimum viable product. The key experiences are parents can receive updates and photos of their kids from the teacher throughout the day. They can sign up for teacher/parent conference right in the app. The parents can connect with other parents in the classroom to plan activities.
