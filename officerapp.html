SCRAMnet Mobile App

The Problem

The probation officers are constantly on the move, and so are their clients. They needed a way to manage the alerts that their clients have generated while being away from their desks.

The Solution

A mobile app solution can give the officer the flexibility to keep moving but still keep a watchful eye on their caseload and clients.

The Project

SCRAM Systems' first mobile application as an extension to the desktop experience for the probation officers. Being very empathetic to the audience, a probation officer, we needed to build a solution that would fit their daily routines but also technology acumen. Through research and feedback, we focus on key tasks and provide an efficient and intuitive workflow on the mobile app.
