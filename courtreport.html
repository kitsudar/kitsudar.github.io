Graphical Court Reports

The Problem

Provide a better way to communicate the large amount of data while making it easy to understand.

The Solution

Create a graphical report template that can be used to communicate the data, compliance and product usage that can be easily intepret and visually appealing.

The Project

With massive amount of data from the products and services the company provides, we needed a way to communicate the data effectively by telling a story and making the data easy to understand. I worked with the report writer/developer and designed a template to use in SSRS where data are pulled in to generate the reports.
